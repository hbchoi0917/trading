## 7. Key Insights

### ✅ What's Working
1. **Put credit spreads on large-caps** (IWM, COST, AVGO, TSLA, CRWD) are the core alpha generator
2. **Multi-account diversification** — same underlying across Individual + Rollover IRA + Roth IRA multiplies premium without increasing per-account risk
3. **H2 2025 discipline** carried into 2026 — consistent execution from Sep 2025 through Feb 2026 (6-month win streak)
4. **Ticker expansion** — new names in 2026 (CRWD, ORCL, AMZN, JPM, BA) improved diversification and reduced single-name concentration
5. **SPXW index spreads** (ROTH IRA) added in 2026 — higher premium with no assignment risk

### ❌ What Needs Improvement
1. **GS structural risk** — $820+ strike spreads have been top losers in both Q4 2025 and Q1 2026. Spread width needs to increase to $20+ or strikes moved lower
2. **IONQ repeat pattern** — high-volatility small-cap with insufficient premium vs. risk. Consider exiting this name entirely
3. **PLTR call exposure (2025)** — selling covered calls near ATM during parabolic moves cost −$3,661
4. **April 2025 drawdown (−$6,854)** — positions too close to ATM during tariff-driven volatility spike; same macro risk exists entering Q2 2026 (tariff announcements Apr 2, 2026)
5. **Call selling overall**: PUT P&L dominates; CALL strategy remains a net drag
